EAAI-23 is Collocated with AAAI-23

Washington, DC, USA

February 7-14, 2023

The Thirty-Seventh AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI-23) will be held February 7 - 14th in Washington, DC, USA.

AAAI-23 Call for Papers

The AAAI-23 Call for Papers is coming soon.

Timetable for Authors

  • July 4, 2022: AAAI web site open for author registration
  • July 11, 2022: AAAI web site open for paper submission
  • August 8, 2022: Abstracts due at 11:59 PM UTC-12 (anywhere on earth)
  • August 15, 2022: Papers due at 11:59 PM UTC-12 (anywhere on earth)
  • February 7-14, 2023: AAAI 2023

The purpose of the AAAI conference series is to promote research in artificial intelligence (AI) and foster scientific exchange between researchers, practitioners, scientists, students, and engineers in AI and its affiliated disciplines. AAAI-23 is the Thirty-Seventh AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence. Like previous AAAI conferences it will feature technical paper presentations, invited speakers, workshops, tutorials, poster sessions, senior member presentations, competitions, and exhibit programs, all selected according to the highest standards. AAAI-23 will also include additional programs for students and young researchers.
